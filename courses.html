Under-Graduation

There are a variety of undergraduate courses offered by Mumbai University, including BA, BSc, B.Com, B.Tech, BMM, BMS, BBI, B.Ed, LLB, and many others. For all undergraduate courses, class 12th exam scores in the relevant subjects with a certain aggregate are the minimum requirements.

Some of the courses like BA and BCom students selection is through merit scores, and there is no entrance for these courses. Rest courses like BSc, BTech, LLB, and B.Ed entrance clearance are a must. The new academic session will begin in July 2023. The applicants who want to take admission can visit the official website www.spdtcollege.ac.in

Post Graduation

In addition to ME, MTech, MA, M.Com, MSc, MSc IT, M.Ed, and LL.M, Mumbai University also offers postgraduate courses in several other disciplines. Applicants must have completed their graduation in a relevant subject at the degree level in order to enroll in these courses.


In some specializations, admission is based on performance in entrance examinations, while in others, it is based on merit. Mumbai University Admissions will begin in July 2023.
